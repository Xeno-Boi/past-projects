Controls:
Camera: 
	WASD to move
	Hold shift to move quicker
	Scroll mouse wheel to zoom
	Right Click + drag to rotate

Friendly Units:
	click to select a unit
	hold space while clicking multiple units to select them at once
When friendly units are selected:
	Click on ground to instruct units to move there
	Click on an enemy (unit or building) to instruct units to attack
	Click on a resource node to instruct units to gather
Buildings:
	buildings can be selected from the HUD and placed in world, at the cost of resources. 
	Must be placed within range of a pyre

Objective: 
Follow the quests. Gather rare resources to build the final building (The World's New Fire) to win the game.
